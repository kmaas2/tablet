Meet Our Founder: Kevin Maas

Meet Our Founder

Kevin Maas is the visionary behind Lakeside Property Masters, bringing his passion for exterior cleaning and his unyielding commitment to quality service to the forefront. Over four years ago, Kevin embarked on his journey in the industry, starting in his hometown of Holland, Michigan.

With a deep understanding of the importance of exterior maintenance for both residential and commercial properties, Kevin has cultivated his expertise and honed his skills over the years. His dedication to achieving impeccable results is evident in every project he undertakes.

As Lakeside Property Masters continued to grow, Kevin recognized the need to assemble a team of professionals who share his dedication to excellence. With a crew of highly skilled and knowledgeable individuals in various specialized fields, Lakeside Property Masters offers a comprehensive range of exterior cleaning and maintenance services.

Kevin and his team now proudly serve locations across the state of Michigan, offering a diverse portfolio of services, from window cleaning and roof repair to holiday lighting. The company's reputation for exceptional workmanship and exceptional customer service is a testament to Kevin's commitment to perfection.
